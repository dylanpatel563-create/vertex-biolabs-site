Good to know

Testing, answered

What does “third-party tested” mean here?

An independent lab — not us — tests each lot by HPLC and mass spectrometry for identity and purity, and screens for contaminants. Results are recorded on a certificate of analysis tied to that lot number.

How pure are your compounds?

Our compounds test to 99%+ purity. The exact figure for your specific lot is printed on its certificate of analysis, which we’ll provide on request.

Are these products approved for human use?

No. All Vertex Biolabs compounds are sold strictly for in-vitro laboratory and research use. They are not for human or veterinary use and are not approved by the FDA for any therapeutic purpose.

Can I get a CoA before I order?

Yes — contact us with the compound you’re interested in and we’ll share a representative certificate of analysis for the current lot.
