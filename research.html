Certificates of analysis

How to read the numbers above

Each result comes from Janoshik Analytical, an independent third-party lab — not from us. Here’s what each field means.

1Purity %

The share of the sample that is the target compound, measured by HPLC. Higher means fewer impurities — our standard is 99%+.

2Batch / Lot #

The unique ID for that production run. It links the certificate to the exact vial, so results are traceable.

3Test method

HPLC separates and quantifies the compound; mass spectrometry confirms its molecular weight and identity.

4Sample tested

The compound and label strength that was submitted to the lab for analysis.

5Measured content

The actual mass of compound found in the vial — confirming it meets or exceeds the labelled amount.

6Independent lab

Every result is issued by a third-party analytical lab, not by us — so the numbers are unbiased, and your vial’s own CoA ships with your order.
