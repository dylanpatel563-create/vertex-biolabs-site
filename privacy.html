Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vertex Biolabs (“Vertex Biolabs,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website, place an order, or contact us. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Order details: your name, email address, phone number (optional), shipping address, and the items in your order.
  • Messages: anything you include when you contact us or submit a form.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical and usage data (such as browser type, device, and pages viewed) that our hosting provider records to operate and secure the site.
  • Your cart is stored locally in your own browser; it is not sent to us until you submit an order.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

How we use your information

  • To confirm stock, process and fulfill your order, and send you an invoice and order updates.
  • To respond to your questions and provide support.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • To keep records and comply with our legal obligations.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run our business:

  • Service providers that help us operate the site and fulfill orders (for example, our website host and form-processing provider, and shipping carriers).
  • Legal and safety reasons — when required by law, or to protect our rights, customers, or the public.

Order and contact submissions are processed through our website host’s form service and delivered to us by email.

Data retention

We keep order and contact records for as long as needed to fulfill orders, support customers, and meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, after which we delete or anonymize them.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by emailing hello@vertexbiolabs.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws; we will honor valid requests as required by law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Age and intended audience

Our site and products are intended only for qualified researchers who are at least 21 years old. The site is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and we will update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email hello@vertexbiolabs.com.
