Return & Refund Policy

Returns, refunds, damaged shipments, and how to get help.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

We want you to be confident in every order. This policy explains when items can be returned, how refunds work, and what to do if something arrives damaged or incorrect. Because our products are research materials, some safety-based limits apply.

30-day returns on unopened vials

Unopened, intact, and unused vials in their original condition may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Please contact us first to start a return — see “How to start a return” below. Once we receive and inspect the item, we’ll arrange a refund or replacement.

Items that can’t be returned

  • Vials that have been opened, reconstituted, or used, for safety and product-integrity reasons.
  • Returns requested more than 30 days after delivery.
  • Items not in their original, intact condition (unless they arrived that way — see below).

Damaged, defective, or incorrect orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order reference and clear photos. We’ll make it right with a free replacement or a full refund, including any return shipping in these cases.

Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method or channel after we receive and inspect the returned item (or confirm a damaged/incorrect order). Please allow a few business days for the refund to be processed; the time it takes to appear depends on your bank or payment provider.

Return shipping

For standard 30-day returns of unopened vials, you’re responsible for return shipping costs unless we agree otherwise. For damaged, defective, or incorrect orders, we cover return shipping.

Order cancellations

Because orders ship quickly, contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or change an order. We can usually accommodate changes before the order has shipped.

How to start a return

Email hello@vertexbiolabs.com with your order reference, the item(s) involved, and the reason for the return (and photos, if the item is damaged or incorrect). We’ll reply with next steps and a return address.
