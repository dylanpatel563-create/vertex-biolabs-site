FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything researchers ask us most: purity, certificates of analysis, shipping, storage, bulk kits, ordering, and returns. Still stuck? Email hello@vertexbiolabs.com or visit our contact page.

Are these products safe for human use?

No. Every Vertex Biolabs compound is sold strictly for in-vitro laboratory and research use. They are not for human or veterinary consumption, are not drugs, supplements, or food, and are not FDA-approved for any diagnostic or therapeutic purpose.

How pure are your peptides?

Our compounds test to 99%+ purity by HPLC and mass spectrometry. You can see the exact purity for each compound on the Research page, and the precise figure for your lot is printed on its certificate of analysis.

Does every order include a certificate of analysis?

Yes. A third-party CoA tied to your lot number ships with every order, free. Need it first? Ask and we’ll send a representative CoA for the current lot before you buy.

How fast and how much is shipping?

Most orders dispatch within 24 hours. U.S. shipping is free over $60 and a flat $5 otherwise, with tracking on every order.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship within the United States only.

How should I store the compounds?

Lyophilized (freeze-dried) vials are best kept cool and dark, preferably refrigerated or frozen (−20 °C) for long-term storage. Once reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, keep refrigerated at 2–8 °C and use within the window noted on each product page.

Do you offer bulk or kit pricing?

Yes. Bulk is sold in kits / sets of 10 vials at a reduced per-vial price, with lot reservations available. Online, single products also include automatic bundle pricing: buy 3+ vials and save. Contact us for a kit quote.

How do I place an order?

Add items to your cart from any product page, choose your quantity, and head to checkout with your shipping details. We confirm stock and email you an invoice with payment instructions within one business day; your order ships within 24 hours of payment.

What is your return policy?

Unopened, intact vials can be returned within 30 days. Reach out and we’ll arrange a refund or replacement.
